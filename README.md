Company Analysis – 2026

This repository contains a comprehensive business analysis based on 2026 data, including product performance, revenue trends, discounts, and marketing insights. The materials are intended to provide insights into company operations, sales patterns, and customer behavior.

📂 Repository Contents
Presentation Slides – Detailed analysis of revenue, product performance, discounts, and marketing strategies.
Graphics & Charts – Visualizations supporting the analysis, including revenue trends and discount correlations.
Raw Data / Example Datasets – If included, sample data used for analysis.
🎯 Purpose

This repository aims to:

Analyze product sales and revenue trends over time.
Evaluate the impact of discounts on revenue.
Identify key periods of high consumer activity (e.g., Black Friday, Christmas).
Provide actionable insights for marketing and sales strategy.
📈 Key Insights
Revenue increases during Black Friday and Christmas, showing high customer activity.
Discount amount vs. revenue correlation is minimal, while discount percentage vs. revenue shows a moderate negative trend.
Current discount policies may need revision to maximize profits.
