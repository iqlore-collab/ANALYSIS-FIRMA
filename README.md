Product Catalogue & Graphics Analysis (2016)

This repository contains the product catalogue and graphics materials created in 2016. It is designed to showcase product visuals and provide insights into marketing and sales materials.

📂 Repository Contents
Graphics – High-quality images and graphics of products.
Product Catalogue – PDF/Slides with product descriptions, prices, and specifications.
🎯 Purpose

The main goal of this repository is to:

Provide a visual reference for all products from 2016.
Serve as a catalogue for marketing and sales purposes.
Offer insights for graphic design and presentation standards.
